The Peacock's Humble Heart

Once upon a time, in a peaceful meadow, a proud peacock walked around, flaunting its stunning feathers. All the animals admired the peacock's beauty, but the peacock never stopped to notice the beauty around it.

One day, the peacock came across a wise old owl sitting on a tree branch. "Why do you stay so still, wise owl? You should be out enjoying the admiration of others like I do," said the peacock.

The owl, with a soft hoot, replied, "I have seen the world and understand that beauty is not just about appearance. It is about how you make others feel, how you treat the world around you."
