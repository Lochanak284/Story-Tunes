The Forgotten Medal

Amulya and Bilvika were the best of friends, always competing in school races. One year, Amulya won the gold medal, while Bilvika came second. Amulya noticed Bilvika was upset but didn't say anything.

Years later, Amulya found the old medal while cleaning her room. Feeling nostalgic, she called Bilvika and invited her over.
