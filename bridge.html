The Bridge Of Trust

Rishi and Nikil were childhood friends who did everything together. One day, they had a big argument over a misunderstanding, and both stopped talking to each other. Days turned into weeks, and the silence between them grew.

One morning, Nikhil found a carpenter working on a bridge over the creek that separated their houses. Confused, Nikhil approached the carpenter and asked what he was doing. The carpenter replied, "Your friend asked me to build this bridge."

Rishi walked onto the bridge and said, "I wanted to fix the broken. I am sorry for the misunderstanding." Touched, Nikhil apologized too, and they shook hands in the middle of the Bridge. From that day on, they vowed never to let a small fight break their bond again.

MORAL: Building Bridges of understanding is more important than winning arguments.
